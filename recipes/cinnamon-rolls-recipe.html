Cinnamon Rolls

Tastes just like Cinnabon!

This cinnamon roll recipe is my absolute favorite. They taste just like cinnabon with their warm gooey cinnamon center and their cream cheese glaze.

If you haven't made cinnamon rolls before, you must try these. This recipe is easy to follow, simple, and will make people think you're a professional baker.

Ingredients

Dough

  • 4c Flour
  • 2 1/4 tsp. active dry yeast
  • 1c milk lukewarm
  • 1/2c granulated sugar
  • 1/3c butter, unsalted, softened, or margarine
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 2 eggs

Filling

  • 1c brown sugar
  • 3 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1/3c butter unsalted, softened

Cream Cheese Icing

  • 6 tbsp butter unsalted, softened
  • 1 1/2c powdered sugar
  • 1/4c cream cheese softened
  • 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 1/8 tsp salt

Steps

  1. For the rolls, dissolve the yeast in the warm milk with a tsp of sugar (you can take a tsp out of the 1/2 cup of sugar) in a large bowl. Let it sit for about 5 minutes. If the yeast is good, it will start to froth up.
  2. To the bowl of your mixer add the sugar, butter, salt, eggs, and flour. Mix until well incorporated.
  3. Pour the milk/yeast mixture over the flour mixture and using the dough hook, mix well until well incorporated and the dough comes clean from the side of the bowl.
  4. Place the dough into an oiled bowl, cover and let rise in a warm place about 1 hour or until the dough has doubled in size.
  5. Combine the brown sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  6. Greast a 9x13 inch baking dish with cooking spray or butter
  7. Roll the dough out on a lightly floured surface, until it is approximately 16in long by 12in wide. It should be approx 1/4in. in thickness.
  8. Spread the 1/3c butter evenlt over the dough, then sprinkle with the sugar evenly over the surface of the dough.
  9. Working carefully, from the long edge, roll the dough down to the bottom edge.
  10. Cut the dough into 1 1/2in. slides. Place in a lightly greased baking pan. You can also use floss to cut into slices. Cover with a clean kitchen towel or plastic wrap and let them rise for 30 min, or until doubled in size.
  11. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  12. Place the baking pan in the oven and bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown.
  13. Whiel the rolls are baking, make the icing by mixing all the ingredients and beat well with an electric mixer until fluffy and smooth.
  14. When the rolls are done, spread generously with icing.
